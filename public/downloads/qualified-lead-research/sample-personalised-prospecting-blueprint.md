# Sample Personalised Prospecting Blueprint

## Product
Qualified Lead Research and Prospecting Blueprint

## Fictional buyer context
Northstar Growth Studio is a fictional boutique consultancy used for public education. The targeting criteria, sample companies and names in this sample are invented.

## Ideal prospect
Smaller online community owners with roughly 100-1,000 members who sell coaching, consulting or professional services and already show a visible booking, consultation or application funnel.

## Priority inclusion criteria

1. Public community or audience-size signal near the target range.
2. Coaching, consulting, advisory or professional service offer.
3. Clear founder, owner or decision-maker identity from public professional sources.
4. Visible business funnel such as a booking page, application form or work-with-me page.
5. Enough public information to write a useful fit note without private data.

## Disqualification criteria

- Celebrity-led or extremely well-known communities.
- No identifiable operator after reasonable public checks.
- No service offer or commercial intent.
- No booking, consultation, application or enquiry path.
- Records that require private personal data to evaluate.

## Suggested research sources

- Public community directories.
- Industry newsletters and podcast guest pages.
- Conference and event speaker lists.
- Public professional profiles linked from business websites.
- Partner directories and service marketplaces.

## Example scoring model

| Factor | Weight | What strong evidence looks like |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Member range fit | 20% | Clear public signal near 100-1,000 members |
| Offer relevance | 20% | Coaching, consulting or professional service offer |
| Funnel clarity | 20% | Booking, application or work-with-me path |
| Owner clarity | 15% | Founder or operator is identifiable |
| Public information quality | 15% | Enough professional information for a clean research note |
| Strategic niche fit | 10% | Priority niche for the buyer offer |

## Outreach angle themes

- Cleaner pipeline quality for service-led communities.
- Better prioritisation before scaling partnerships or outbound.
- Turning existing booking/application interest into a more systematic research queue.

## Confidentiality note
This sample is fictional and does not include private contact information, private telephone numbers, personal email addresses or performance claims.
