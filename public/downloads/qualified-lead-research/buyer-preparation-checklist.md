# Buyer Preparation Checklist

Use this before buying or completing the questionnaire for the Custom Lead Research and Prospecting Blueprint.

## Have ready

- Business name and website or best public profile.
- One-sentence description of the offer.
- Target industries or 3-5 possible priority niches.
- Target locations or remote/global preference.
- Preferred company size, team size or audience range.
- Decision-maker roles you usually need to reach.
- At least three must-have prospect characteristics.
- At least three exclusions or poor-fit patterns.
- Main sales objective: calls, applications, partnerships, research handoff or positioning clarity.
- Preferred outreach channel.

## Do not send

- Private customer lists you are not allowed to share.
- Other people's private phone numbers.
- Personal email databases.
- Confidential client information.

## Better inputs create sharper output
Short, concrete examples help more than broad labels. Instead of "small businesses", write the specific kind of buyer, business model, funnel or situation that makes someone a strong fit.
